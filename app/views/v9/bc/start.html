Add your organisation’s bank account details

We need your organisation’s bank account details, so that we can make your hire a new apprentice payment.

You can only add one bank account for your organisation. We’ll use this account to make all of your payments.

The payment is different to the apprenticeship levy. You can use it for anything to support your organisation’s costs, including to pay for recruiting and employing new apprentices.

Before you start

You’ll need to give us your:

  • contact information
  • organisation’s address and bank account details
We may contact a person with significant control to verify the information you provide.
