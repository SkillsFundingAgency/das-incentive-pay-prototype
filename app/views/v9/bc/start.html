Set up to get payments from ESFA

In order to receive funding and payments from the DfE and its associated bodies or agencies you will need to provide details of your organisation to the DfE Standing Data team.

Please note, we will maintain one set of banking details for your organisation which will be used for all payments from the DfE and its associated bodies or agencies.

If you have any queries please contact the DfE Standing Data team directly by emailing DfE.StandingData@education.gov.uk.
