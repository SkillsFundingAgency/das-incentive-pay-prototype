What happens next

You’ll receive the first payment after the apprentice completes 90 days of their apprenticeship and the second payment after 365 days.

We make payments on the 10th working day of each month, using your organisation’s bank details. You do not need to do anything else.

Changes to your payment

Your payment amount may change if an apprentice has their 25th birthday before they start their apprenticeship.

We cannot make your payment if:

  • an apprentice is on a break in learning
  • an apprentice’s contract of employment ends before they complete 90 or 365 days of their apprenticeship
  • the training provider does not confirm that the apprentice has started their apprenticeship
  • the apprentice started their contract of employment before 1 August 2020
