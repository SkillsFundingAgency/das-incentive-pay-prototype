Apply for incentive payments

Incentive payments are available for employers that take on new apprentices from September 2020. You can apply for the incentives after you add each apprentice to your apprenticeship service account.

There are 2 incentives available for apprentices:

  • aged 16 to 24
  • who will spend at least X weeks on off-the-job training at the start of their apprenticeship

You can only get incentive payments for apprentices that are new to your payroll.

What you'll get

The amount you’ll get for apprentices:

  • aged 16 to 24 is £3,000 (paid in 2 equal instalments 90 and 365 days after you apply)
  • who will spend at least X weeks on off-the-job training is £2,500 (paid in the second month of the apprentice’s training)

If you apply for multiple eligible apprentices, we’ll pay their incentives together.

Apprentices who were made redundant

You can take on and apply for incentive payments for apprentices who were made redundant by their previous employer. This includes apprentices who will either:

  • start a new apprenticeship standard
  • continue with their previous apprenticeship standard
