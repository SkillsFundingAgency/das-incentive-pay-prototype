What happens next

We cannot send any payments until we’ve received and verified {{_orgNamePlural}} organisation and finance details. This could take up to 90 days and we may contact someone from {{_orgName}}.

You can add {{_orgNamePlural}} organisation and finance details:

  • using the link we’ve sent to your email address
  • in the ‘hire a new apprentice payment applications’ section of the apprenticeship service account

After we’ve verified {{_orgNamePlural}} organisation and finance details

We’ll send the payment in 2 equal instalments for each apprentice. We’ll send the:

  • first payment after an apprentice completes 90 days of their apprenticeship
  • second payment after an apprentice completes 365 days of their apprenticeship

The training provider needs to confirm to ESFA when an apprentice is ready for each payment.

Payments are sent by Bacs on the 14th working day of the month. It usually takes 3 working days to process.
