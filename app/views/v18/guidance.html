Apply for the hire a new apprentice payment

{% if myData.closing == "true" %}

We’re closing applications for the hire a new apprentice payment

You need to apply before 30 April 2021.

{% endif %}

The hire a new apprentice payment is available for employers that take on new apprentices from 1 August 2020 to 31 March 2021. Employers can apply for the payment after they add new apprentices to their apprenticeship service account.

The payment is in addition to the existing £1,000 an employer will already get for taking on an apprentice who is either:

  • aged 16 to 18 years old
  • under 25 and has an education, health and care plan or has been in the care of their local authority

What you’ll get

For apprentices aged:

  • 16 to 24, employers will receive £2,000
  • 25 and over, employers will receive £1,500

We’ll make the payment in 2 equal instalments for each apprentice. The first payment is after an apprentice completes 90 days of their apprenticeship and the second is after 365 days.

Payments are made by Bacs on the 14th working day of the month. It usually takes 3 working days to process.

We cannot make any payments until we’ve received and verified {{_orgNamePlural}} organisation and finance details. This could take up to 3 months and we may contact someone from {{_orgName}}.

What you can use the payment for

{{_orgName}} can use the payment for anything to support their costs.

The payment is different to the apprenticeship levy and {{_orgName}} can use it to pay for recruiting and employing new apprentices.

Eligibility

{{_orgName}} cannot apply for an existing employee who joined their organisation before 1 August 2020, even if they started an apprenticeship after this date.

{{_orgName}} can only apply for new apprentices who joined their organisation from 1 August 2020 to 31 March 2021.

Before you start

You'll need to add the apprentices you want to apply for to the apprenticeship service account.

You can add {{_orgNamePlural}} organisation and finance details when you apply, or someone else from {{_orgName}} can add them later. This includes {{_orgNamePlural}}:

  • legal name, registered address and contact details
  • Companies House number (if {{_orgName}} has one)
  • VAT number (if {{_orgName}} has one)
  • remittance email address
  • finance director or finance lead’s contact details
  • bank account details
