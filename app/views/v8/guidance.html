Apply for the new apprentice payment

The new apprentice payment is available for employers that take on new apprentices from 1 August 2020 to 31 January 2021. Employers can apply for the payment after they add new apprentices to their apprenticeship service account.

This is in addition to the existing £1,000 payment we already offer for new apprentices:

  • aged 16 to 18 years old
  • under 25 with an education, health and care plan

What you'll get

For apprentices aged:

  • 16 to 24, employers will receive £2,000
  • over 25, employers will receive £1,500

You’ll get the payment in 2 equal instalments, after the apprentice completes 90 and 365 days of their apprenticeship.

We make payments on the 10th working day of each month. You’ll need to give us your organisation’s bank details when you apply, so that we can pay you directly.

What you can use the payment for

You can use the payment for anything to support your organisation’s costs.

The payment is different to the apprenticeship levy and you can use it to pay for recruiting and employing new apprentices.

Eligibility

You can only apply for this payment for new apprentices who started their contract of employment between 1 August 2020 and 31 January 2021.
