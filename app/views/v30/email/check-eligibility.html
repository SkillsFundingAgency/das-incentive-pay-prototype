Dear EMPLOYER LTD,

During the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic, you’ve recruited an apprentice and have applied for the hire a new apprentice payment. Thank you for your support in upskilling the UK workforce.

We’re working to make the payments to you in early 2021.

We understand that businesses have had to adapt and change quickly due to the impact of coronavirus. To make sure our payments are accurate, we need you to:

  • check that all of the apprentices you’ve applied for are eligible
  • find out what to do if you’ve applied for an apprentice who is not eligible

Who is eligible

You can only apply for new apprentices who joined your organisation from 1 August 2020 to 31 March 2021.

You cannot apply for an existing employee who joined your organisation before 1 August 2020, even if they started an apprenticeship after this date.

How to check

You can check which apprentices you’ve applied for in your apprenticeship service account (https://accounts.manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk).

What to do if an apprentice is not eligible

Contact us if you have applied for any apprentices who are not eligible for the payment. You can:

  • call us on 0800 0150 600 (option 1 then option 2)
  • email us on helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk

We’ll continue to audit applications. If you have provided any inaccurate or misleading information, you may have to repay any relevant hire a new apprentice payments.
