Check apprenticeship details when applying for the incentive payment for hiring a new apprentice

EmployerThis article sits within the Employer section

If your incentive payment application has the ‘Confirm eligibility’ status, you may need to check:

  • your employer PAYE scheme and/or 
  • the apprentice’s National Insurance number

If this information is incorrect, you need to update it and confirm with our help desk so we can re-check your application.

We will stop incentive payments and reclaim any funds paid for apprentices who do not match our eligibility criteria. If an apprentice is not eligible, you need to cancel your application.

Check and update a PAYE scheme

  1. Check with the apprentice’s training provider that they have saved the correct National Insurance number on their account.
  2. If it is incorrect, ask the training provider to update it.
  3. Contact our help desk so we can re-check your application: helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk.

Check and update an apprentice’s National Insurance number

  1. Check with the person who adds PAYE scheme numbers to your employer’s account that they have saved the correct one. A PAYE reference number is 11 characters. Find out more about adding a PAYE scheme number to your account in this video.
  2. If it is incorrect, ask them to update it.
  3. Contact our help desk so we can re-check your application: helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk.

Other evidence:

You need to keep the following documentation for each apprentice as you may be asked to provide evidence to confirm your claim is valid: 

  • a full signed copy of the employment contract 
  • a full signed copy of the aaprenticeship agreement and commitment statement (if you are unsure of what these are, please discuss with your training provider)
  • payroll evidence, for example, a copy of a payslip 

If you are applying for someone you previously hired as part of the Kickstart Scheme, we may email you and ask for additional once your application has been processed.

This evidence could include:

  • the Kickstart Scheme grant agreement
  • proof of the start date of employment through Kickstart Scheme (this could be a copy of employment contract or a letter of appointment)
  • the first and last payslip issued under the Kickstart Scheme to the kickstarter
  • evidence of the receipt of Kickstart Scheme grant payment

If required, we may ask you for evidence in addition to the list above to help determine eligibility.

Eligibility dates

Ensure that you have only applied for eligible apprentices.

For apprentices with an employment start date from 1 October 2021 to 31 January 2022:

  • you can apply from 11 January – 15 May 2022
  • the apprentice must be a new employee, with a contract of employment with a start date between 1 October 2021 and 31 January 2022 (inclusive) 
  • the apprentice must not have a contract of employment with you (or a connected company or charity) in the 6 months prior to 1 October 2021, unless you can evidence that prior employment was under the Kickstart scheme, as detailed in the funding rules
  • the apprentice must have an apprenticeship start date between 1 October 2021 to 31 March 2022 (inclusive) 

For apprentices with an employment start date from 1 April 2021 to 30 September 2021:

  • applications are closed
  • the apprentice must be a new employee, with a contract of employment with a start date between 1 April 2021 and 30 September 2021 (inclusive) 
  • the apprentice must not have a contract of employment with you (or a connected company or charity) in the 6 months prior to 1 April 2021, unless you can evidence that prior employment was under the Kickstart scheme, as detailed in the funding rules

the apprentice must have an apprenticeship start date between 1 April 2021 and 30 November 2021 (inclusive) 

For apprentices with an employment start date from 1 August 2020 to 31 March 2021:

  • applications are closed
  • the apprentice must be a new employee, with a contract of employment with a start date between 1 August 2020 and 31 March 2021 (inclusive) 
  • the apprentice must not have a contract of employment with you (or a connected company or charity) in the 6 months prior to 1 August 2020
  • the apprentice must have an apprenticeship start date between 1 August 2020 and 31 May 2021 (inclusive)
