You need to accept the employer agreement

To apply for the hire a new apprentice payment, all employers must review and accept an agreement with the Education and Skills Funding Agency.

The version you need to accept depends on whether you've already accepted the agreement we published on 9 January 2020. If you have:

  • already accepted the 9 January 2020 agreement, you only need to accept a variation to it now
  • not accepted the 9 January 2020 agreement yet, you need to accept a new agreement
