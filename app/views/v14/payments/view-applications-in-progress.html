       Why your payment date is an estimate

Before we can make a payment, your training provider must confirm that the apprentice has completed 90 or 365 days of their apprenticeship.

Training providers usually submit their apprentice information to ESFA at the start of each month. Your payment date can change if your training provider:

  • does not confirm that your apprentice started their apprenticeship
  • does not submit their information on time
  • confirms a different apprenticeship start date
  • gives us information that’s different to your apprenticeship service account (also known as a ‘data lock’)
