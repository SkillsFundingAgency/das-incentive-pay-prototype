Subject: New financial support for apprenticeships

Introducing the recruit a new apprentice grant


Hi there,

As the UK adjusts to the new normal, apprenticeships offer an exciting opportunity to encourage growth. Hiring an apprentice allows you to fill key skill gaps, giving your organisation a competitive edge as the economy starts to recover. 

And new financial support is available to help you on your apprenticeship journey. 

The recruit a new apprentice grant is available for employers taking on new apprentices from August 2020. You’ll receive money from the government to support your organisation’s costs during the coronavirus pandemic, including recruiting and employing your apprentice.
